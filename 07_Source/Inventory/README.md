# Inventory

Registers and inventories that track **what custom assets exist, where they
live, and their version-control / export status.**

## What belongs here

- The [Asset Register](ASSET_REGISTER.md) — the master list of custom assets.
- Future inventories (e.g. a font/licence inventory) if genuinely useful.

## What does not belong here

- The assets themselves (those live in `Themes/`, `CSS/`, `Exports/`, etc.).
- General project documentation — that belongs in `01_Documentation/`.

> The register is the quickest answer to "is this safe to change, and is it
> backed up?" Keep it current as assets are brought under version control.
